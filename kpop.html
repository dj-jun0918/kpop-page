Evolution of K-Pop

Gen 1
1990s

The Beginning

It started with Seo Taiji and Boys, followed by the first idol groups like H.O.T. and S.E.S. laying the foundation of the trainee system.

Gen 2
2000s

The Golden Era

K-pop begins its expansion across Asia. Noteable names include BIGBANG, Girls' Generation, and Super Junior creating the "Hallyu" wave.

Gen 3
2010s

Global Domination

Social media propels K-pop to the West. BTS and BLACKPINK shatter glass ceilings, performing at major US award shows and stadiums.
